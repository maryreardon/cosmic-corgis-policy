Privacy Policy for Cosmic Corgis

Effective Date: October 31, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Cosmic Corgis! This privacy policy explains how we handle information in our mobile game. Your privacy is important to us, and our policy is simple: we do not collect any of your personal information.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

Cosmic Corgis is designed to be a completely offline, standalone game. We do not collect, store, or have access to any personally identifiable information (PII) from your device. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Your name, email address, or phone number.
  • Your device's unique ID, advertising ID, or IP address.
  • Your location data.
  • Your contacts, photos, or other files on your device.
  • Any usage data or analytics about how you play the game.

3. How Game Data is Stored

All game progress, including your resources (Star Kibble, spins, shields) and building status, is stored exclusively on your local device. This data is not transmitted to us or any third party. If you uninstall the game, this data will be permanently deleted from your device.

4. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The game is designed to be safe for all ages as it has no data collection or online interaction features.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
